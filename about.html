About Us

Global North and South Movement on Climate Action (GNSMCA)

  • About GNSMCA

    Founded in 2021, the Global North and South Movement on Climate Action is a global movement of youth and youth networks from the Global North and South striving to create unified voice perspectives around five main thematic focus areas. Working with several institutional partners from Academia, Community-Based Organizations, Non-Governmental and Inter-governmental Agencies, Private Sector Actors, we believe the climate crisis is a global concern and can only be addressed by joint action and unified stances.

  • Who We Are ?

    We are young people from vast backgrounds both from the global North and the global South who met at COP 26 and COP 27, realized a gap concerning climate action; how other young people at the Grassroots are also brought on board for their voices to be heard and hence we decided to have the global North and Global South Dialogue on climate action that will help achieve this objective and also have a broader impact concerning climate action. Despite all our organizations and entities that we represent we decided to have this dialogue for a global North and Global South as young people but not as the organizations that we represent.

  • Our Work and Mandate

    Climate Change negotiations at different levels including UNFCCC COP still continue to yield minimal results and progress due to non-consensus between parties majorly the Global North and Global South but also due to lack of political will among others. Through our global dialogue series, we bring forth intergenerational dialogues and perspectives, fostering robust and intense learning, steering collaboration, building solidarity to understand the systemic climate risks and challenges affecting our individual communities.

  • Participation

    We are to have regional representatives at least two from each continent, and these regional representatives are to work hand in hand with the country representatives at least one from each country. We are also to take on a multi-sectoral approach; working with some key professionals in the climate space from various backgrounds like faith institutions, school/universities, private sector, among other sectors.

  • Objectives:

    (1) .To amplify and unify the voices of Climate Activists globally with the purpose of having a shared voice towards climate action
    (2) .To institutionalize, build the capacity of climate activists and advocates around the World to understand the systemic climate issues, lead on local climate action; on mitigation, adaptation and nationally determined contribution concepts for climate just communities.
    (3) .To influence, through a unified voice, World Leaders to unite and act swiftly towards establishment of clear safeguards and mandates to fast-track the implementation of the Paris Agreement and several other international conventions.
