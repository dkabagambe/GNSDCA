Global North and South Movement

Whereas climate change remains a hard pressing issue around the World, the younger generation; children and youth are increasingly showing resilience, setting up the pace to lead on action and commitments to keep the global average way below 2 degrees. With the need to foster collaboration between youth from the Global North and South, the Global North and South movement is creating a global wave and momentum for joint knowledge sharing, collaboration and synergies around four main pressing climate change issues of concern. Through this movement, we are creating a unified voice among youth for climate action on four thematic areas; Climate finance/ Loss and Damage, Nature/Nature based Solutions, Just energy transition and Climate Mobility/ Migration
